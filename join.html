FAQ

Common questions.

Do I have to be a CS major?

Nope. CS, IT, CIS, math, engineering, even art majors learning to code — all welcome. Curiosity beats credentials here.

Is there a GPA requirement?

None. We're a community, not an honor society. If anything, struggling classes is the best reason to join — we run study groups every week.

What if I've never coded before?

Perfect. Many of our members started exactly there. We run beginner workshops the first Wednesday of every month, and our mentors are happy to start from absolute scratch.

Do I need national ACM membership?

No, it's optional. You can attend everything we host as a free chapter member. National ACM ($20/yr) just adds the Digital Library, O'Reilly, and Skillsoft on top.

Can I attend a meeting before joining?

Yes — please do! All meetings are open. Just walk in: Oxendine Science 1234, Wednesdays at 6:00 PM.
