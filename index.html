Why join

More than a club. A launchpad.

From your first "Hello, World" to your first internship offer, the chapter is here for every step.

Hands-on workshops

Git, Linux, web dev, Python, AI — learn the tools the bootcamps charge for, free, every week.

Mentorship network

Connect with alumni working at Google, Microsoft, Lockheed Martin, Red Hat, and more.

Hackathons & comps

We organize HackUNCP each spring and travel to regional competitions like HackNC and HackDuke.

Career & internship prep

Resume reviews, mock interviews, and Leetcode nights — we'll get you ready before recruiters arrive.

ACM membership perks

Access to the full ACM Digital Library, free O'Reilly books, and Skillsoft tech courses.

Real community

Discord, study groups, game nights, and a faculty-supported home base for every CS major at UNCP.
